M1 motorway reopens after alert
Part of the M1 motorway in Belfast which had been closed due to a security alert has now reopened.A number of controlled explosions were carried out on a suspicious vehicle at Blackstaff Way in the Kennedy Way industrial estate.The road had been closed in both directions between Stockman's Lane and Broadway.There is understood to be a backlog of traffic coming from the Lisburn direction as a result of the alert.
